# OrionTabulation Privacy Policy

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use OrionTabulation.

- Data controller
  - OrionTabulation, Inc. (contact: oriontabulation@gmail.com)

- What we collect
  - Account information (name, email, organization)
  - Usage data (features used, session duration, error logs)
  - Device and technical data (IP address, browser, OS)
  - Optional data you provide (feedback, support requests)

- How we use your data
  - Provide and improve the service
  - Personalize your experience
  - Monitor, diagnose, and fix issues
  - Communicate about updates and support

- Data sharing
  - With service providers (hosting, analytics) under strict obligations
  - With you, when requested (data access, export)
  - When required by law or to protect rights

- Data retention
  - Retain data for as long as your account is active or as needed for the service
  - Anonymize or delete data when you delete your account or upon request per legal requirements

- Security
  - Industry-standard measures (encryption in transit and at rest, access controls)
  - Ongoing security reviews and updates

- Your rights
  - Access, correct, delete your data
  - Data portability and restriction of processing where applicable
  - Withdraw consent where applicable (without affecting service)

- Cookies and tracking
  - We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the service and track usage
  - You can manage cookie preferences in your browser

- International transfers
  - Transfers may occur to jurisdictions with different data protection laws, safeguarded by standard contractual clauses

- Children
  - Not intended for children under 16; we do not knowingly collect data from them

- Changes to this policy
  - We’ll notify you of material changes and obtain your consent when required

- How to contact us
  - oriontabulation@gmail.com
